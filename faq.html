Frequently Asked Questions

All You Wanted to Know And More




DO YOU OFFER GIFT CARDS?

Yes! We do offer Gift Cards at our shop. They expire within 2 years of purchase and need to be present at time of redemption. Call our shop to purchase one today.


DO YOU HAVE AN ONLINE STORE?

We do not yet have an online store. If you would like to purchase directly from us please call or send an email, and we can help you find the perfect book or gift. However we do have affiliate websites with bookshop.org for books and Libro.fm for Audio-books. Proceeds from your purchases help our store.


DO YOU SHIP ITEMS?

We do offer shipping within the Continental United States at the USPS shipping rates. If the shipment is books only, we offer Media Mail rates.


WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

We do offer exchanges or store credit for returns with the following conditions:

  1. 1. The book must be in excellent condition,
  2. 2. Receipt must be present,
  3. 3. Return is completed within 30 days of purchase.
