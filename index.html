Welcome to Bean Bag Books

About Us

Beanbag Books was originally started as the teacher supply store, Fundamentals Parent-Teacher Store. We have been a neighborhood favorite for over 30 years. This children's bookstore is now under new ownership since November 2018. New owners Michael and Jody Everett are thrilled to be a part of the MainStreet Delaware community and to keep this independent bookstore a part of this vibrant town!
